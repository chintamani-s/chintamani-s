About Me

I am a highly skilled Senior Software Engineer with over seven years of proven experience specializing in embedded systems for high-performance industrial automation and medical devices. My expertise includes software architecture, real-time operating systems, and Agile methodologies, which provides a robust foundation for building efficient, scalable embedded systems.

Currently, as a Senior Embedded Engineer at Schneider Electric, I lead the technical implementation of drive controller firmware infrastructure, coordinating with cross-functional teams to deliver integrated solutions for industrial automation systems. My work involves architecting Yocto-based build systems, developing C++ application components for OPC UA FX and EtherCAT protocols, and establishing automated testing frameworks.

My background in industrial automation and embedded Linux development provides a unique perspective on integrating complex software solutions with real-world hardware. I have a track record of optimizing embedded drivers, working with FPGA acceleration, and developing hybrid Linux/RTOS architectures, skills that are directly applicable to developing high-performance, real-time control systems.

I am passionate about leveraging my combined expertise in embedded systems engineering and DevOps automation to solve challenging technical problems and deliver innovative, impactful solutions across various domains, from industrial automation to medical imaging systems.
